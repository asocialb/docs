# Audio-Video Content Erasing Template

The Smart Erasing function is off by default.This document offers instructions on how to associate a  Audio-Video Content Erasing template with a specific push domain to activate the Audio-Video Content Erasing feature and how to disassociate the template to deactivate Audio-Video Content Erasing.

## Notes

- A template comes into effect approximately 5-10 minutes after it is associated.
- After the template is successfully associated, the smart erase function will be enabled for the push URL under the specified push domain.
- Only one Audio-Video Content Erasing template can be associated with a domain. Once associated, all streams under that domain will be Audio-Video Content Erasing in accordance with the associated template.

## Prerequisites

- You have successfully logged in to the [CSS console](https://console.tencentcloud.com/live/livestat) and have completed [Adding Your Own Domain](https://www.tencentcloud.com/document/product/267/35970).
- The Audio-Video Content Erasing template has been successfully created.

## Associating Audio-Video Content Erasing Template

1. Go to [Domain Management](https://console.tencentcloud.com/live/domainmanage). Click **Domain Name** you want to configure or **Manage** to enter the domain details page.

![](https://staticintl.cloudcachetci.com/cms/backend-cms/9991e87dc5b711f0a7ca5254001c06ec.png)

2. Select the **Template Configuration** tab. Click**Edit**in the top-right corner of  **Audio-Video Content Erasing Template**.

![](https://staticintl.cloudcachetci.com/cms/backend-cms/311727b23c3011f0b0ce5254007c27c5.png)

![](https://staticintl.cloudcachetci.com/cms/backend-cms/c91690b600b711f1b5c0525400380f7d.png)

3. Choose a smart erase configuration template according to your business requirements and click **Confirm** to complete the configuration.

![](https://staticintl.cloudcachetci.com/cms/backend-cms/eeec32d500b711f1b5c0525400380f7d.png)

## Unbinding Audio-Video Content Erasing Template

1. Go to [Domain Management](https://console.tencentcloud.com/live/domainmanage). Click **Domain** **Name** you want to configure or **Manage** to enter the domain details page.

![](https://staticintl.cloudcachetci.com/cms/backend-cms/a17e9bfdc5b711f086d9525400e889b2.png)

2. Select the **Template Configuration** tab and click Edit in the top-right corner of **Audio-Video Content Erasing Template**.

![](https://staticintl.cloudcachetci.com/cms/backend-cms/8cdf3d553c3011f09bbe525400454e06.png)

![](https://staticintl.cloudcachetci.com/cms/backend-cms/021e580500b811f198c8525400370dda.png)

3. Based on your business requirements, uncheck the relevant template and click **Confirm** to proceed.

![](https://staticintl.cloudcachetci.com/cms/backend-cms/15a1987300b811f1b5c0525400380f7d.png)

> **Note:**Unbinding a Audio-video content erasing template does not affect ongoing live streams.
