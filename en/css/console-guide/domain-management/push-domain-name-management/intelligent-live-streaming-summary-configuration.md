# Intelligent Live Streaming Summary Configuration

The  Intelligent Summary function is off by default.This document offers instructions on how to associate a Intelligent Summary template with a specific push domain to activate the  Intelligent Summary feature and how to disassociate the template to deactivate Intelligent Summary.

## Notes

- A template comes into effect approximately 5-10 minutes after it is associated.
- After the template is successfully associated, the intelligent summary function will be enabled for the push URL under the specified push domain.
- Only one intelligent summary template can be associated with a domain. Once associated, all streams under that domain will be intelligent summary in accordance with the associated template.

## Prerequisites

- You have successfully logged in to the [CSS console](https://console.tencentcloud.com/live/livestat) and have completed [Adding Your Own Domain](https://www.tencentcloud.com/document/product/267/35970).
- The intelligent summary template has been successfully created.

## Associating  Intelligent Summary Template

1. Go to [Domain Management](https://console.tencentcloud.com/live/domainmanage). Click **Domain Name** you want to configure or **Manage** to enter the domain details page.

![](https://staticintl.cloudcachetci.com/cms/backend-cms/e521558bc5b711f0a7ca5254001c06ec.png)

2. Select the **Template Configuration** tab. Click **Edit** in the top-right corner of **Intelligent Live Streaming Summary Configuration**.

![](https://staticintl.cloudcachetci.com/cms/backend-cms/325e67f5c5bb11f086d9525400e889b2.png)

![](https://staticintl.cloudcachetci.com/cms/backend-cms/47f9f52ec5bb11f0a7ca5254001c06ec.png)

3. Choose a intelligent summary configuration template according to your business requirements and click **Confirm**to complete the configuration.

![](https://staticintl.cloudcachetci.com/cms/backend-cms/7cb13782c5bb11f0a2c65254005ef0f7.png)

## Unbinding Intelligent Summary Template

1. Go to [Domain Management](https://console.tencentcloud.com/live/domainmanage). Click **Domain** **Name** you want to configure or **Manage** to enter the domain details page.

![](https://staticintl.cloudcachetci.com/cms/backend-cms/ec5244b3c5b711f0a7ca5254001c06ec.png)

2. Select the **Template Configuration** tab and click Edit in the top-right corner of **Intelligent Live Streaming Summary Configuration**.

![](https://staticintl.cloudcachetci.com/cms/backend-cms/8a3f8d2cc5bb11f0a62b5254007c27c5.png)

![](https://staticintl.cloudcachetci.com/cms/backend-cms/9d47b855c5bb11f0b88b525400bf7822.png)

3. Based on your business requirements, uncheck the relevant template and click **Confirm** to proceed.

![](https://staticintl.cloudcachetci.com/cms/backend-cms/6d0e8170c5bb11f086d9525400e889b2.png)

> **Note：**Unbinding a intelligent summary template does not affect ongoing live streams.
