# Delayed Playback to Become Paid Feature

Delayed playback will become a paid feature starting from June 15, 2023. See below for the pricing and billing details.

## Must-Knows

- In addition to extended feature fees, using the delayed playback feature will also incur [traffic/bandwidth costs](https://www.tencentcloud.com/document/product/267/2819).

## Pricing

| Type | Unit cost |
| --- | --- |
| Price (USD/Billing Unit) | 0.01515 |

**Ratio of billing duration to actual duration:**

| Extended Feature | RatioBilling Duration (Billing Unit) : Actual Duration (Minutes) |
| --- | --- |
| Delayed playback | 0.05 : 1 |

## Billing Details

- Item: Extended feature
- Billing mode: Pay-as-you-go.
- Billing cycle: Daily. The fee generated each day will be deducted from your account the following day. For the actual fee deduction and bill generation time, see your billing statement.

## Calculation Formula

- Billing duration = Actual duration x Ratio
- Fee = Unit price x Billing duration

## Billing Example

- Suppose you used the delayed playback feature for 60 minutes on June 15, 2023. On June 16, the following extended feature fee would be billed:

0.01515 (USD) x 0.05 (billing unit/minute) x 60 (minutes) = 0.04545 (USD)

The above billing rules will take effect at 00:00 on June 15, 2023. The delayed playback fee incurred on June 15 will be billed on June 16. Please pay attention to your account balance and make any changes you deem necessary in a timely manner. If you have any questions, feel free to contact us.
