# Terminal SDK

## Overview

Media Processing Service (MPS) Top Speed Codec (TSC) terminal SDK can provide features like encoding compression, audio enhancement, and video super resolution on user clients (such as mobile phones, computers and tablets), improving the overall media quality and experience.

## Prerequisites

The prerequisites for purchasing or trying the MPS terminal SDK are as follows:

1. You must have [signed up for a Tencent Cloud account](https://www.tencentcloud.com/zh/account/register?s_url=https%3A%2F%2Fconsole.tencentcloud.com%2F) and completed identity verification.
2. The account must have the permissions to make purchases on the MPS product page.

## Directions

Log in to the [Tencent Cloud MPS console](https://console.tencentcloud.com/mps/client_sdk/license) and go to the MPS product **Terminal SDK License** page.

![](https://staticintl.cloudcachetci.com/cms/backend-cms/89fb3f8ea00e11efa04c52540055f650.png)

### Applying for Trial **Terminal SDK License**

#### Step 1: Self-applying for Trial SDK License

On the page, click on **Create Trial License**, select the SDK type and version needed for the trial, and continue to click **Create License** to complete the application.

![](https://staticintl.cloudcachetci.com/cms/backend-cms/d66d2b63a00e11efaaca525400fdb830.png)

> **Note:**Each trial License is valid for 60 days. Within the validity period, you cannot re-apply for multiple SDK Licenses of the same type and version.

#### Step 2: Binding Application Information

The trial SDK License you have applied for is inactive and will become active only after the application information binding is completed.

![](https://staticintl.cloudcachetci.com/cms/backend-cms/fda1220fa00e11efaaca525400fdb830.png)

Click **Bind** and fill in the corresponding application information to complete the binding process.

![](https://staticintl.cloudcachetci.com/cms/backend-cms/0be67ebaa00f11efa646525400329841.png)

> **Note:**With the trial validity period, you can modify the binding information indefinitely. Once the license is upgraded to an official paid License, the binding information cannot be changed at will. Please confirm the binding information before upgrading to the official License.

### Purchasing Terminal License

#### Step 1: Selecting the Product to Be Purchased

On the page, click **Create Terminal SDK License** and you will be guided to the purchase page, where you can select the SDK License you need to purchase. Select the following in turn:

1. License Type: TSC - Terminal Audio SDK, TSC - Terminal Enhancement SDK.
2. Billing Mode: Annual or monthly purchase.
3. SDK Version.
4. Duration.
5. Number of Licenses (usually 1).

![](https://staticintl.cloudcachetci.com/cms/backend-cms/8b45de44a00f11efa0b3525400bdab9d.png)

#### Step 2: Confirming Order Payment

After selecting the required SDK configuration, click **Purchase Now** to view the product information under this payment. After submitting the order, choose an appropriate payment method to complete the payment.

![](https://staticintl.cloudcachetci.com/cms/backend-cms/b9ae2c78a00f11efaaca525400fdb830.png)

#### Step 3: Binding App Information

On the purchased License, fill in the App binding information. After confirmation, the service will take effect.

> **Note:**The binding information can only be filled in once. If there is a mistake, please contact Tencent Cloud to [submit a ticket](https://console.intl.cloud.tencent.com/workorder/category) for modification.

![](https://staticintl.cloudcachetci.com/cms/backend-cms/cb4366eca70c11ef804852540055f650.png)

### Upgrading from Trial Edition to Official Edition

#### Step 1: Upgrading to Official License

On the page, click **Upgrade to Official License** for the target trial License to upgrade and purchase the License.

![](https://staticintl.cloudcachetci.com/cms/backend-cms/246801dba01011efa0b3525400bdab9d.png)

> **Note:**After upgrading to Formal License, the binding information cannot be modified. Please check and confirm the binding information before purchasing.After the official License is purchased, the trial period will automatically end.

![](https://staticintl.cloudcachetci.com/cms/backend-cms/2ee7b59ba01011ef992f52540075b605.png)

#### Step 2: Selecting the Billing Type and Duration

Click **Purchase Prepaid SDK**, then you can choose to purchase on a monthly or yearly basis. Click **Next** to enter the order confirmation page. After payment, the License will be upgraded to the official edition.

![](https://staticintl.cloudcachetci.com/cms/backend-cms/52f28e72a01011ef834b525400f69702.png)
