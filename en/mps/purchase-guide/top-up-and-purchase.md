# Top Up and Purchase

## Daily Pay-As-You-Go

If you use the [daily pay-as-you-go billing mode](https://www.tencentcloud.com/document/product/1041/49204#daily-billing), fees are charged based on your actual usage of MPS features. Please pay attention to your account balance and top up in a timely manner.

1. Log in to the Tencent Cloud console and click [Billing Center](https://console.tencentcloud.com/account/recharge) in the top-right corner. Select **Payment Management > Payment** on the left sidebar.
![](https://staticintl.cloudcachetci.com/cms/backend-cms/5398c261d6d811ee9409525400c26da5.png)
2. Enter the top-up amount, and click **Pay Now**.

## Monthly Pay-As-You-Go

For [monthly pay-as-you-go](https://www.tencentcloud.com/document/product/1041/49204#monthly-billing), the unit prices specified in your contract with Tencent Cloud will apply. For more information, please contact your sales rep.

## Prepaid Resource Packs

1. Log in to the [MPS console](https://console.tencentcloud.com/mps). On the overview page, click **Buy resource pack** to go to the [purchase page](https://buy.tencentcloud.com/mps).
2. Select the resource packs you want to buy.

> **Note:**You can buy multiple resource packs. The one that expires first will be used first.Each resource pack has its own validity period. If you buy multiple packs, their validity periods will not be added up.Each MPS resource pack can only deduct a specific type of MPS resources.After all your resource packs are used up, you will be automatically switched to the [pay-as-you-go](https://www.tencentcloud.com/document/product/1041/49204) mode.
