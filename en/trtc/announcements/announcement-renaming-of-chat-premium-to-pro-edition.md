# Announcement: Renaming of Chat Premium to Pro Edition

**Dear Chat Users,**

Thank you for your continued support of our Chat product. To provide clearer distinction between our product editions, we have decided to rebrand the Premium edition of our Chat service.

Effective January 2025, the ***Premium*** edition will officially be renamed the ***Pro*** edition. We kindly ask that you take note of this change and update any relevant references accordingly.

Please be assured that this is purely a branding update; it will not affect the features, pricing, or any other aspects of the service. Your business operations will remain unaffected by this change.

We appreciate your kind understanding as we continue to enhance our product offerings.

**Best regards,**

**Tencent RTC Team**
