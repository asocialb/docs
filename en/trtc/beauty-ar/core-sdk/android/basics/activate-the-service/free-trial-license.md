# Free Trial License

To enhance your experience with the Beauty AR SDK feature, we offer a free trial for each SDKAppID, allowing free access to 4 editions:**All Beauty Features, Virtual Background, Face Recognition and Gesture Recognition,**as well as the capability of **Gift AR**. On the mobile platform, each SDKAppID can enjoy each feature **for free once (with each trial lasting 14 days, renewable once for a total of 28 days).**

This document provides instructions and guidance on [Applying for a trial license](#mobileapplytrial) and [Renewing the trial license](#mobilerenewtrial) for Beauty AR SDK Mobile and Gift AR.

## Apply for a trial license

You can activate the free trial of Beauty AR SDK in the TRTC console. The specific steps are as follows:

1. Log in to [TRTC Console > Relevant Services](https://console.trtc.io/aboutcloud), and click **Get Started.**

![](https://cloudcache.intl.tencent-cloud.com/cms/backend-cms/845a3da805f911efa2e5525400488742.png)

2. Select **Mobile**, and based on your actual needs, fill in the `App Name`, `Package Name`, and `Bundle ID`. Check the features you want to trial: **All Beauty Features, Virtual Background,  Face Recognition, Gesture Recognition, Gift AR**, then click **Confirm**.

![](https://cloudcache.intl.tencent-cloud.com/cms/backend-cms/1fef7185516f11f0adb15254005ef0f7.png)

3. After activation, you can view your information on the current page and refer to the **Integration Guide** above for integration. The `License Url` and `License Key` will be used in the [**integration guide**](https://trtc.io/document/60195).

> **Noteï¼**For integrating gift animation special effects, please refer to: [Gift AR (Android)](https://www.tencentcloud.com/document/product/1143/70531)and [Gift AR (iOS)](https://www.tencentcloud.com/document/product/1143/70537).

![](https://cloudcache.intl.tencent-cloud.com/cms/backend-cms/7a5c4d522faa11f08caa5254005ef0f7.png)

## Renew the trial license

The trial license initial application comes with a default validity period of 14 days, after which you can renew **once**. Click on the **Renew** button at the bottom of the package information box, and then click **Confirm** to renew the feature for another 14 days.

![](https://cloudcache.intl.tencent-cloud.com/cms/backend-cms/a5ac67822fcc11f09e67525400bf7822.png)

## Upgrade the trial license

If you need to upgrade the trial License of Beauty AR SDK Web&H5 to an official license to extend its validity period, please first [select and purchase the Beauty AR SDK package for Mobile](https://console.trtc.io/subscription/buy/beauty-mobile), then follow these steps:

1. Click the **Upgrade** at the bottom of the trial license package information box.

![](https://cloudcache.intl.tencent-cloud.com/cms/backend-cms/8e3683e92fcc11f0b44b5254007c27c5.png)

2. Enter the **Upgrade interface**, click **Bind**, select an unbound Beauty AR SDK Mobile package, and click **Confirm** to upgrade and create an official application with the same package name, while unlocking the official license of Beauty AR SDK without needing an issuance review. If there are no bindable Beauty AR SDK packages, you can go to the [Beauty AR SDK Purchase Page](https://console.trtc.io/subscription/buy/beauty-mobile) to make a purchase. **Please note that resources will take effect immediately after purchase, and the validity period will begin.**

![](https://cloudcache.intl.tencent-cloud.com/cms/backend-cms/b7e41f162fcc11f091625254001c06ec.png)
