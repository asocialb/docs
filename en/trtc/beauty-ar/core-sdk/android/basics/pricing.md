# Pricing

The Beauty AR SDK for Mobile is divided into 3 series: **Standard editions (A series), advanced editions (S series), and Capabilities.** They differ in features. You can choose one that best fits your needs.

- [Standard A series packages](https://www.tencentcloud.com/document/product/1143/69949#Standard-A-editions) offer basic beautification features and are suitable for customers who have basic requirements for facial beauty adjustment.
- [Advanced S series packages](https://www.tencentcloud.com/document/product/1143/69949#Advanced-S-Editions) offer advanced beautification features (including stickers and makeup effects) and are suitable for customers with high requirements for facial beautification.
- [Capabilities](https://www.tencentcloud.com/document/product/1143/69949#X-Series-Capabilities) offers Tencent Effect services at the capability level, to give you greater flexibility and scalability.

The Beauty AR SDK packages can be purchased on an annual basis. Among them, the basic A series packages A1 - 00, A1 - 01, A1 - 02, and A1 - 03, and the Portrait keying, Gift AR can be purchased on a monthly basis. For pricing details, see [Pricing](https://www.tencentcloud.com/document/product/1143/69949#2d4c8749-8cc3-466d-b918-a9a6b8c4ad08).

You need to [purchase](https://console.trtc.io/subscription?pkgType=beautyAR&subType=mobile) different licenses (packages) to use different editions of the Beauty AR SDK.

## Billing Details

- **The licenses will become active immediately after you purchase them.Please bind the package name in the console as soon as possible after purchase.**
- One Beauty AR license can be bound to one mobile application. A license can be bound to one bundle ID and one package name. Modifications are not allowed after binding. If you need to use Beauty AR with multiple applications, you need to purchase multiple licenses.
- After a license expires, you need to purchase a new license; otherwise, the corresponding features will become unavailable.
