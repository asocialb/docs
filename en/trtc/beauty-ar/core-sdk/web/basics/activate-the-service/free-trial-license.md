# Free Trial License

To enhance your experience with the Beauty AR SDK feature, we offer a free trial for each SDKAppID. On the Web&H5 platform, each AppID can enjoy a free trial for 14 days, with an option to extend once, totaling 28 days.

This article provides guidance on [Applying for a trial license](#webapplytrial), [Renewing the trial license](#webrenewtrial), and [Upgrading the trial license](#webupgradetrial) for Beauty AR SDK Web&H5.

## Apply for a trial license

You can activate the free trial of Beauty AR SDK Web&H5 in the TRTC Console. The specific steps are as follows:

1. Log in to [TRTC Console > Relevant Services](https://console.trtc.io/aboutcloud), and click **Get Started.**

![](https://cloudcache.intl.tencent-cloud.com/cms/backend-cms/f9c49b2605fb11efa2e5525400488742.png)

2. Select **Web&H5**, and fill in the `Project Name`and `Domain` as per your situation. Once completed, click **Confirm**.

![](https://cloudcache.intl.tencent-cloud.com/cms/backend-cms/898d79ea0c3011ef9ef35254002977b6.png)

3. After activation, you can view the information on the current page, and refer to the **Integration Guide** below for integration. The `License Key` and `License Token` will be used in the[integration guide](https://trtc.io/document/50099).

![](https://cloudcache.intl.tencent-cloud.com/cms/backend-cms/f9d0f9c505fb11efa2e5525400488742.png)

## Renew the trial license

The trial License is initially valid for 14 days by default. After it expires, you can renew it **once** by clicking **Renew** at the bottom of the package information box, then clicking **Confirm** to renew the feature for another 14 days.

![](https://cloudcache.intl.tencent-cloud.com/cms/backend-cms/f9b5dbb705fb11ef8a9c5254003e1eb1.png)

## Upgrade the trial license

If you need to upgrade the trial License of Beauty AR SDK Web&H5 to an official license to extend its validity period, please first [select and purchase the Beauty AR SDK package for Web&H5](https://console.trtc.io/subscription/buy/beauty), then follow these steps:

1. Click the **Upgrade** at the bottom of the trial license package information box.

![](https://cloudcache.intl.tencent-cloud.com/cms/backend-cms/f9c32d8a05fb11efa2e5525400488742.png)

2. Enter the **Upgrade interface**, click **Bind**, select an unbound Beauty AR SDK Web&H5 package, and click **Confirm** to upgrade and create an official application with the same package name, while unlocking the official license of Beauty AR SDK without needing an issuance review. If there are no bindable Beauty AR SDK packages, you can go to the [Beauty AR SDK Purchase Page](https://console.trtc.io/subscription/buy/beauty) to make a purchase. **Please note that resources will take effect immediately after purchase, and the validity period will begin.**

![](https://cloudcache.intl.tencent-cloud.com/cms/backend-cms/f9c60faf05fb11ef9aef5254009d370c.png)
