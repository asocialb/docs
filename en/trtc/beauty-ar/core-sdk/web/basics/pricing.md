# Pricing

Beauty AR SDK Web&H5 has 3 packages: **Standard, Professional and Advanced**. The packages can be purchased on a monthly basis. The Standard version can unlock the capabilities of beauty, filters, makeup, 2D stickers and virtual backgrounds. In addition to the corresponding capabilities of the Standard version, the Professional version can unlock the advanced capabilities of 3D stickers, Animoji expressions and virtual images.

You can choose a package based on your specific business needs, and purchase the appropriate package by visiting the [Beauty AR SDK purchase page](https://console.trtc.io/subscription?pkgType=beautyAR&subType=web) to unlock the corresponding features.

### Billing Details

- Each license can be bound to one domain. **For official licenses, modifications are not allowed after binding**. **You can use localhost for testing purposes**. If you want to use Beauty AR for multiple domains, you need to buy multiple licenses.
- About domains:
  - **An absolute domain name** refers to a fixed domain address, such as: `a.com`, `a.b.com`, `a.b.c.com`, etc., which are unique and fixed domain addresses.
  - **A wildcard domain name** refers to a series of domain addresses with the same domain suffix, such as the wildcard domain `*.a.com`. After binding the license, domain names with the same domain suffix, such as `a.a.com`, `b.a.com`, `c.a.com`, etc., can unlock the package capability. The `*` position can support the customization of multi-level domain names, such as `b.c.a.com`, `b.c.d.a.com`, etc.
- When a license expires, to continue using Beauty AR, you need to renew the license. To avoid interrupting your business, we recommend you enable auto-renewal. For details, see [Adding and Renewing a License (Web)](https://www.tencentcloud.com/document/product/1143/60213#webrenewal).
- **The materials offered by a package can only be used with one license.** We prohibit all forms of material exchange, including but not limited to gifting, resale, and renting. Tencent Cloud reserves the right to take legal action against violators.
- About free materials: The free materials we provide will be continuously updated. You can also create the sticker effect you want on your own.
