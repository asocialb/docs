# Product Introduction

## Overview

Beauty AR SDK is a powerful and versatile tool designed to enhance beauty experiences. It offers a wide range of features including face quality detection, skin smoothing, virtual makeup, face shaping, virtual backgrounds, AR stickers and more. With its advanced augmented reality technology, developers can integrate realistic beauty filters and effects into their applications, providing users with an immersive and personalized beauty enhancement experience.

In addition, Beauty AR also provides the capability to play gift animations, supporting efficient and smooth playback after converting various live gift formats. For details, please refer to the [Gift AR](https://www.tencentcloud.com/document/product/1143/70595).
