# Relay to CDN

The statistics of the TRTC console's Relay to CDN usage are mainly used to display the details of the bypass push service provided by TRTC. If you use the third-party repush billing provided by the CSS, go to the [CSS console](https://console.tencentcloud.com/live/livestat) to view the statistics.

## Notes

The data is updated every 5 minutes instead of in real time, so there may be a delay of 5-180 minutes in the display of statistics.

## Directions

1. Enter the [Tencent RTC console > Usage](https://console.trtc.io/statistics) and select **Relay to CDN**.
2. Select the app you want to view, then select the time period (using bandwidth billing mode as an example).

![](https://cloudcache.intl.tencent-cloud.com/cms/backend-cms/383d69accb6911f08e74525400bf7822.png)

## Usage Details

The detailed records show peak bandwidth in five-minute intervals or daily peaks, with monthly peaks used for billing. Estimate your monthly costs by examining the detailed logs, but refer to the [Billing Center](https://console.tencentcloud.com/expense/bill) for the actual charges.

![](https://cloudcache.intl.tencent-cloud.com/cms/backend-cms/3cd3095dcb6911f0a4a55254001c06ec.png)
