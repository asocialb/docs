# Free Minutes

All Tencent Cloud accounts (UIN) that have activated the TRTC monthly package (including Call, Conference, Live, RTC-Engine, Conversational AI) can receive a free time package of 10,000 minutes each month within a year. A maximum of one free time package can be received per month, and the usage generated by all TRTC (including Call, Conference, Live, RTC-Engine,Conversational AI) can be deducted.

> **Notes on free time package:**The remaining free minutes for each month will expire on the last day of the current cycle and cannot be used in the next cycle.For example, if you receive the free minutes on November 15, 2022, they would be valid from November 15, 2022 to December 14, 2022.You can check the validity of your free minutes in [Package Management](https://console.trtc.io/package) of the TRTC console.If the deducted minutes do not exceed 10,000 minutes, the service is free for the current month. If the 10,000-minute Free Quota is deducted and no [RTC-Engine Packages](https://www.tencentcloud.com/document/product/647/56025), [Call Monthly Packages](https://www.tencentcloud.com/document/product/647/54632), [Conference Monthly Packages](https://www.tencentcloud.com/document/product/647/59409), or[Live Monthly Packages](https://www.tencentcloud.com/document/product/647/59407) has been purchased, it will trigger the suspension of the application. The service cannot be automatically restored after suspension, you need to [purchase a package](https://console.trtc.io/subscription) to reactivate it. Once the service is restored, the complimentary free minutes can still be used.
