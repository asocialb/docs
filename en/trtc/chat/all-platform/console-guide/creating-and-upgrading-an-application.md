# Creating and Upgrading an Application

## Use Cases

This document describes how to create Free Trial apps and obtain SDKAppIDs. It also covers how to upgrade Free Trial apps to Standard or Pro edition or Pro Plus edition or Enterprise edition in the Chat console.

## Prerequisite

You have signed up for a [Tencent Cloud account](https://trtc.io/zh/register?s_url=https://console.trtc.io) and set a payment method.

## Creating a Developer Edition App

1. Log in to the [Chat console](https://console.trtc.io). Click **Create Application**.

![](https://cloudcache.intl.tencent-cloud.com/cms/backend-cms/7250abd4ca9c11f087ad52540099c741.png)

2. Enter the application name in the create pop-up window and choose **Chat**, select the appropriate **Deployment Region**, then click **Create**.

![](https://cloudcache.intl.tencent-cloud.com/cms/backend-cms/7efdb625ca9c11f09e745254007c27c5.png)

3. Upon creation, you can view the status, business version, SDKAppID, SDKSecretKey, creation time, and expiration time of the newly created application on the current [**Chat Product Details Page**](https://console.trtc.io/chat) or [**My Applications**](https://console.trtc.io/app).

> **Note:**By default, a new app is of Free Trial and enabled.A Tencent Cloud account can create a maximum of 300 Chat apps. If you want to create a new app, [disable and delete](https://www.tencentcloud.com/document/product/1047/34540) an unwanted app first. **Once an app (along with its SDKAppID) is deleted, the service it provides and all its data are lost. Proceed with caution**.

![](https://cloudcache.intl.tencent-cloud.com/cms/backend-cms/12e82695ca9d11f0b011525400bf7822.png)

1. Click **Edition** and view **current version** feature support detail within the frame.
2. Click **Version comparison** to view all version comparisons.

![](https://cloudcache.intl.tencent-cloud.com/cms/backend-cms/281af04eca9d11f0b011525400bf7822.png)

3. Click **Upgrade Plan**, go to purchase page and choose the version to upgrade. After purchase completion, you can go to [**Chat Product Details Page**](https://console.trtc.io/chat) or [**My Applications**](https://console.trtc.io/app) to view application info.
