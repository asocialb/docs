# Official Channel Management

Log in to the [Chat Console](https://console.trtc.io/), and in the left navigation bar, select [Chat > Configuration > Official Channel Management](https://console.trtc.io/chat/public-account-management). You can manage official channels according to your actual business needs. You can also manage official channels by calling the corresponding REST APIs. For detailed instructions, see the [Official Channel Management API Documentation](https://www.tencentcloud.com/document/product/1047/60755).

## Create Official Channel

1. On the **Official Channel Management** page, click **Create Official Channel**.

![](https://cloudcache.intl.tencent-cloud.com/cms/backend-cms/4f2d7cf8fc2111f0837f525400ecee81.png)

2. On the **Create Official Channel**window, configure the following parameters:
  - Official Channel Name: Enter a name for the official channel. This parameter is required and must not exceed 150 bytes.
  - Profile Photo: Upload an image to use as the avatar for the official channel. This parameter is required. There are no restrictions on image size or format.
  - Official Channel Profile: Optionally, enter a brief description for the official channel. This parameter must not exceed 400 bytes.

![](https://cloudcache.intl.tencent-cloud.com/cms/backend-cms/2625119efc2311f0a98b52540073fd3b.png)

3. Click **Create** to save the official channel.

## Edit Official Channel

1. On the **Official Channel Management** page, click **Edit**button.

![](https://cloudcache.intl.tencent-cloud.com/cms/backend-cms/f38ddc5ffc2411f0afd0525400370dda.png)

2. In the **Edit Official Channel** window, update the official channel name, description, or photo as needed.

![](https://cloudcache.intl.tencent-cloud.com/cms/backend-cms/f204bbf9fc2511f0a98b52540073fd3b.png)

## Delete Official Channel

1. On the **Official Channel Management** page, select the official channel you want to delete and click **Delete** button on the right.

![](https://cloudcache.intl.tencent-cloud.com/cms/backend-cms/5bf3b086fc2711f0840952540097cba1.png)

2. After clicking **Delete** button, a confirmation window will appear. Deleted official channels cannot be recovered.

![](https://cloudcache.intl.tencent-cloud.com/cms/backend-cms/da67a21cfc2711f084a0525400380f7d.png)

## Create Post

The Post Management page lets you create, view, and search all posts sent from official channels

![](https://cloudcache.intl.tencent-cloud.com/cms/backend-cms/354ac46afcb911f084a0525400380f7d.png)

> **Note:**The Post Management page only displays published posts and their details. Editing posts is not supported.

Steps for Creating posts:

1. **Select Post Type**. Choose the type of post to create. The system supports the following types:
  - Image and Text: Post with images and text.
  - Image Only: Post with images only.
  - Text Only: Post with text only.
2. **Edit Post Content**. Enter the post content in the input box and upload images in the image upload area.
3. **Add Links**. Click **Add Link** to insert hyperlinks for secondary navigation.
4. **Preview Content**. You can preview the post in real time in the preview area on the right.

![](https://cloudcache.intl.tencent-cloud.com/cms/backend-cms/c33ce6f4fc2b11f0afd0525400370dda.png)

5. **Publish Post.** After completing all required fields, click Publish to send the post. Once published, the post will appear in the post list.

![](https://cloudcache.intl.tencent-cloud.com/cms/backend-cms/696cc16efcb511f0837f525400ecee81.png)

6. If the post fails to send, a popup in the upper right corner will show the error message. Update the post as needed based on the prompt.

![](https://cloudcache.intl.tencent-cloud.com/cms/backend-cms/d7a3a351fcb611f084a0525400380f7d.png)
