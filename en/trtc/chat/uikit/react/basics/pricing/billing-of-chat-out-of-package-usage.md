# Billing of Chat Out-of-Package Usage

## Quota of Free Monthly Active User (MAU)

- MAU: Free Monthly Active Users
- Billing method: After invoking the Chat SDK login operation and establishing a long connection with the Chat backend, the MAU will increase by 1. That is, a single user logging into Chat in a month counts as 1 MAU. Repeated logins by the same user do not accumulate MAU.
- Specific price details are shown in the table below:

| Description | Free Trial | Standard | Pro | Pro-plus | Enterprise |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Free Monthly Active Users (MAU) Quota | 100/month | 10,000/month | 10,000/month | 25,000/month | 50,000/month |
| Out-of-plan excess MAU fee | Not supported. | 0.015 USD/MAU/month |  |  |  |

> **Note:**Use the Chat SDK Login operation as needed based on your business scenario to avoid a high MAU.Starting April 1, 2026, **out-of-plan MAU overage fees** will be adjusted. For details on the effective rules and pricing, please refer to the [official announcement](https://www.tencentcloud.com/en/announce/detail/100941).
