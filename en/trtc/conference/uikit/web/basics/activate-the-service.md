# Activate the Service

## Activate Trial Service

To better experience the features of TUIRoomKit, we provide a 14-day trial version for each SDKAppID (the trial version does not include complimentary call duration). Each SDKAppID can try it for free twice, with a valid period of 14 days each time. Meanwhile, the total experience times for all SDKAppIDs under one account are 10.

1. Log in to the [Tencent RTC console](https://console.trtc.io) and click **Create Application**.

![](https://cloudcache.intl.tencent-cloud.com/cms/backend-cms/51e8e2d8c5ea11f0a7ca5254001c06ec.png)

2. In the popup, enter an **application name**, select **Conference**, and choose the appropriate **deployment region**. Then click **Create**. This will create a TRTC application bound to the trial edition of TRTC Room.

> **Note:**The default deployment region for TRTC service data is in Singapore, and the storage for Chat service data is in your selected data center.

![](https://cloudcache.intl.tencent-cloud.com/cms/backend-cms/efae06a5c5ea11f0a7ca5254001c06ec.png)

3. After the application has been created, you will automatically be taken to the Conference product details page. At this point, you have quickly created an application and successfully received the TRTC Conference (TUIRoomKit) trial version. You can view information on the current [**Conference Product Details Page**](https://console.trtc.io/conference) or [**My Applications**](https://console.trtc.io/app), and refer to the [integration guide](https://www.tencentcloud.com/document/product/647/54845) for integration. The `SDKAppID` and `SDKSecretKey` will be used in the integration guide.

![](https://cloudcache.intl.tencent-cloud.com/cms/backend-cms/22259b94c5eb11f086d9525400e889b2.png)

## Purchasing the Official Editions

**You need to purchase the conference monthly package to use TUIRoomKit**. For details on the price and feature comparison of the conference monthly package, refer to [Conference Monthly Package Billing Explanation](https://www.tencentcloud.com/document/product/647/59409).**To purchase, follow these steps:**

1. Visit [Conference Purchase Page](https://console.trtc.io/subscription/buy/conference), select **the application (SDKAppID) and version to purchase**, **and it is recommended to enable auto-renewal to avoid impacting business usage**. After confirming purchase information and agreeing to the relevant agreement, click **Subscribe now**.

![](https://cloudcache.intl.tencent-cloud.com/cms/backend-cms/cd31d325c6a411f09e745254007c27c5.png)

2. Proceed to the payment page to complete payment. After purchase completion, you can go to [**Conference Product Details Page**](https://console.trtc.io/conference) or [**My Applications**](https://console.trtc.io/app) to view application version info. See [integration guide](https://www.tencentcloud.com/document/product/647/54845) to integrate.

## Renew the Official Edition

1. Visit [**Conference Product Details Page**](https://console.trtc.io/conference), click **Renewal / Upgrade**.

![](https://cloudcache.intl.tencent-cloud.com/cms/backend-cms/dc50914dc6a411f0a93d52540044a08e.png)

2. Visit [Conference Purchase Page](https://console.trtc.io/subscription/buy/conference), confirm the application (SDKAppID) to purchase, select the version of the same application package, **and it is recommended to enable auto-renewal to avoid impacting business usage**. Once enabled, when the **account balance is sufficient, it will automatically renew monthly upon expiration**. After confirming purchase information and agreeing to the relevant agreement, click **Subscribe now**.

![](https://cloudcache.intl.tencent-cloud.com/cms/backend-cms/dc528d08c6a411f0b011525400bf7822.png)

3. Proceed to the payment page to complete payment. After purchase completion, you can go to [**Conference Product Details Page**](https://console.trtc.io/conference) or [**My Applications**](https://console.trtc.io/app) to view application version info. See [integration guide](https://www.tencentcloud.com/document/product/647/54845) to integrate.

## Upgrading to Official Edition

1. Visit [**Conference Product Details Page**](https://console.trtc.io/conference), click **Renewal / Upgrade**.

![](https://cloudcache.intl.tencent-cloud.com/cms/backend-cms/f7eac4f6c6a411f0a4a55254001c06ec.png)

2. Visit [Conference Purchase Page](https://console.trtc.io/subscription/buy/conference), confirm the application (SDKAppID) that requires upgrading, select **a higher version than the application package**. After confirming purchase information and agreeing to the relevant agreement, click **Subscribe now**.

![](https://cloudcache.intl.tencent-cloud.com/cms/backend-cms/f7e1112fc6a411f0b011525400bf7822.png)

3. Proceed to the payment page to complete payment. After purchase completion, you can go to [**Conference Product Details Page**](https://console.trtc.io/conference) or [**My Applications**](https://console.trtc.io/app) to view application version info.

## Automatic Renewal

The steps to enable auto-renewal in the console are as follows:

1. Visit [**Conference Product Details Page**](https://console.trtc.io/conference).
2. Select **the application that requires auto-renewal**.
3. In the Conference product information, click the **Auto-renewable** **Enable** button. A confirmation pop-up will appear. Click **Enable**.

![](https://cloudcache.intl.tencent-cloud.com/cms/backend-cms/0ccd8876c6a511f0a4a55254001c06ec.png)
