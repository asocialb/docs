# Product Introduction

**The RTC Room Engine SDK** is a software development kit jointly used by [Group Conferencing (RoomKit)](https://www.tencentcloud.com/document/product/647/54634#) and [Live Streaming and Voice Chat Rooms (TUILiveKit)](https://www.tencentcloud.com/document/product/647/60034#). It supports rich features such as room management, screen sharing, member management, microphone position control, and basic beauty filter, while ensuring 720P and 1080P high-definition quality. It maintains high-quality audio and video transmission even in weak network environments with 70% packet loss. Additionally, the SDK adopts 48kHz high-quality audio sampling, combined with Tencent Ethereal Audio Lab's 3A processing algorithm, to cancel echo and howling, achieving full-link 128kbps high-quality stereo sound, bringing users clear and immersive interaction experience.

## **Group Conferencing (RoomKit)**

The multi-person meeting (RoomKit) is a low-code UI component designed for scenarios such as corporate meetings, webinars, and online education. It supports global connectivity across platforms including mini programs, iOS, Android, Web, uni-app, and Flutter. It offers meeting control features like room management, member management, and screen sharing, with support for various video qualities including standard definition, high-definition, and UHD. By integrating this component, you can add multi-person audio and video session functionality to your app in just three steps within one day, enabling rapid business launch.
