# Live Monthly Packages

TRTC Live (TUILiveKit) is a real-time interactive live streaming component that comes with UIKits. It enables you to add the capability of **Interactive Live Video Streaming and Voice Room**to your app with just a few lines of code, significantly reducing your development workload. TUILiveKit is available for Android, iOS, Flutter, and Electron development platforms.

## Cost Breakdown

TRTC Live fees include Live monthly package fees and pay-as-you-go fees.

- Live monthly package fees: **The TRTC Live component can be used only after you purchase a Live monthly package.**  For package prices and features, refer to [Live Monthly Packages](https://www.tencentcloud.com/document/product/647/59407#658e2423-30d2-45e2-91b8-128b2730b072).
- Pay-as-you-go fees: Usage will be billed in the pay-as-you-go mode after the quota of your Live monthly package is used up,**so please note that even if you subscribe to a monthly package, additional fees may still be incurred.**Pay-as-you-go fees include:
  - Out-of-plan excess MAU fees: Exceeding the quota incurs a charge of $0.015 USD per user per month.
  - [Audio/video duration fees](https://www.tencentcloud.com/document/product/647/42734): The usage generated by the live's video live component is billed according to the [Billing of Video Live Component](https://www.tencentcloud.com/document/product/647/66078); the audio and video duration usage incurred during the use of live's voice room component is billed in accordance with the [Audio and Video Duration](https://trtc.io/document/42734?product=pricing).
  - Additional Value-Added Fees: If you use TRTC basic features, there may also be [on-cloud recording fees](https://www.tencentcloud.com/document/product/647/45176), [recording delivery fees](https://www.tencentcloud.com/document/product/647/45176),[MixTranscoding fees](https://www.tencentcloud.com/document/product/647/47631), and [relay fees](https://www.tencentcloud.com/document/product/647/47631).

## Billing Details

- TRTC Live features are offered at the application (SDKAppID) level. If you have multiple applications that need to use TRTC Live features, you need to purchase multiple TRTC Live monthly packages.
- After the TRTC Live monthly package bound to your application expires,  **the features of the package will become unavailable**. To avoid interrupting your business, we recommend you enable  **auto-renewal** for your purchased monthly package in the [TRTC Console](https://console.trtc.io/overview) (**Select** **Application > Choose Product > Enable Auto-renewal**).
- **You can subscribe to only one TRTC Live package for one application at a time**. For example, for the application  **"test_trtc"**, if you have already subscribed to  **Live Lite**, before the package expires, you cannot upgrade it to  **Live Standard or Pro**. If you do need to upgrade your package, please [submit a ticket](https://console.tencentcloud.com/workorder/category).
- TRTC Live packages must be purchased first before they can be used to deduct your usage. The validity period is from the day of purchase to the same day of the following month. For example, if you purchase a package on March 1, 2023, its validity period will be from March 1, 2023, to April 1, 2023.
- For specific instructions on how to obtain the free trial edition or purchase the official edition, see [Activate the Service (TUILiveKit)](https://www.tencentcloud.com/document/product/647/60033).
- You may refer to the [TRTC Monthly Package Refund Instructions](https://www.tencentcloud.com/document/product/647/58588) for the refund rules regarding the Live monthly packages.
