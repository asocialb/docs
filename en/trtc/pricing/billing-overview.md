# Billing Overview

The service items of Tencent RTC  (including Call, Conference, Live, and RTC-engine)ï¼Chat and Beauty AR are divided into two major categories according to the service type: **monthly subscription** and **pay-as-you-go**.

- **Monthly subscription:**TRTC introduced prepaid package plans starting from **March 7, 2023 (UTC+8),** unlocking different value-added functional services for various applications (SDKAppIDs). The package plans for Call, Conference, Live, Chat, Beauty AR, RTC Engine, and Monitoring Dashboard are all monthly subscription packages. You can choose the appropriate prepaid package plan for your business scenario.

> **Note:**Monthly package subscriptions support automatic renewal. By turning on **Automatic renewal**, you are agreeing to the recurring subscription renewal of the Services. For your convenience, TRTC will be automatically deducting the relevant renewal fees from your account, credit card, debit card or through any other payment methods that you have previously provided to us in the signing up of your account or purchasing of the services. You can cancel and opt-out of such automatic renewal at any time. In that case, payment will be terminated following the expiry of that current subscription term in which you opt out of the renewal.

- **Pay-as-you-go:**TRTC defaults to prepaid package billing. When the service usage cannot be deducted by the package or exceeds the balance of the prepaid package, it will be billed based on pay-as-you-go according to your actual business usage.
