## Manage Packages

### Subscribe to a Package

To subscribe to the Monitoring Dashboard package, follow these steps:

#### **Method 1:**

1. Log in to the [Purchase Page](https://trtc.tencentcloud.com/buy/dashboard).
2. Select the **desired edition** (**Standard** or **Premium**), review and accept the agreement, then click **Buy Now**.
3. Complete the payment as prompted.

#### **Method 2:**

1. Log in to the [Console](https://console.trtc.io/monitor).
2. In the left menu, click **Monitoring Dashboard** > **Room Call Investigation**.
3. Click **Switch to Official Edition** in the top left corner.
4. Select the **desired edition** (**Standard** or **Premium**), review and accept the agreement, then click **Buy Now**.
5. Complete the payment as prompted.

### Cancel Subscription

To cancel your subscription, please [submit a ticket](https://console.tencentcloud.com/workorder/category) and our team will assist you.
